# Problem Statement & System Abstract

## 1. About the Company
**Avinash Kanaparthi Infra Private Limited** is a large-scale civil engineering and infrastructure development firm based in Telangana, India. The company specializes in road construction, bridge works, highway expansions, and government-contracted utility installations. With a portfolio that includes multi-crore public sector and private contracts, the firm manages complex subcontractor networks, multi-stage progress billing, and strict regulatory compliance.

## 2. The Problem
At Avinash Kanaparthi Infra Private Limited, billing disputes with clients (such as government departments or principal contractors) arise frequently due to:
- Discrepancies in quantity measurements (e.g., concrete volume, earthwork excavation depth).
- Differences in rate interpretations or item classifications.
- Penalties applied for project delays, lack of equipment mobilization, or quality test failures.

Currently, resolving these disputes is a slow, manual, and unstructured process. When a client disputes an invoice or issues a debit note, the finance team must compile evidence from scattered sources (such as physical Measurement Books, site logs, test certificates, and emails) and manually draft a dispute resolution or response letter.

### Impact of the Manual Process:
- **Cash Flow Delays**: Manual letter drafting takes days or weeks, delaying dispute resolution and delaying multi-crore invoice payments.
- **Inconsistencies**: Letters lack standard structures, professional phrasing, or concrete references to contract clauses and supporting evidence.
- **Information Scarcity**: Records are scattered across spreadsheets, WhatsApp messages, and paper files, meaning past disputes cannot be tracked or audited.
- **Management Blindspot**: Senior management has no real-time visibility into active billing disputes, total disputed amounts, or the resolution success rate.

## 3. The Solution: AI Client Billing Dispute Resolution Tool
The **AI Client Billing Dispute Resolution Script** is a digital web platform designed to streamline and automate this process. 

### How it Works:
1. **Structured Input**: The finance team inputs the finance details (Project, Client, Invoice details), the dispute parameters (disputed items, rates, quantities), and the supporting evidence (M-Book pages, survey records, correspondence).
2. **AI-Powered Generation**: The system uses a specialized, context-aware prompt (Prompt v4) to construct a comprehensive dispute resolution response letter. The letter is structured professionally, referencing contract obligations, specific quantities, and the uploaded evidence.
3. **Tracking & Analytics**: Every dispute and generated letter is logged in a centralized history database with options for rating, copying, PDF export, and regeneration.
4. **Management Visibility**: An Admin Dashboard displays real-time analytics on billing dispute patterns, total dispute values, feedback quality, and average resolution times.
